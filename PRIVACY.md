# Privacy Policy — YouTube Title Redactor

**Effective date:** July 21, 2026

YouTube Title Redactor ("the extension") is designed to respect your privacy.
This policy explains what the extension does and does not do with your data.

## Summary

The extension does **not** collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal
information or browsing data. Everything it stores stays on your own device.

## What the extension stores

The extension stores only the **list of title keywords you create**. This list
is saved locally on your device using the browser's `chrome.storage.local` API
so that your keywords persist between sessions and are available to the
extension's popup and its on-page functionality.

This keyword list:

- is created and controlled entirely by you;
- never leaves your device;
- is not transmitted to the developer or any third party;
- can be edited or deleted at any time from the extension's popup, and is
  removed if you uninstall the extension.

## What the extension does NOT do

- It does **not** collect personal or identifying information.
- It does **not** track your browsing history or activity.
- It does **not** send any data to remote servers.
- It does **not** use analytics, advertising, or third-party trackers.
- It does **not** contain or load any remote code; all code runs from the
  files included in the extension package.

## Permissions

- **Storage:** used solely to save your keyword list locally on your device.
- **Host access to youtube.com:** used solely to read video titles on YouTube
  pages and, where a title matches one of your keywords, replace the title text
  and cover the associated thumbnail. No page content is recorded or sent
  anywhere.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same
location with a revised effective date.
